# Invite Tracking

Know **who invited whom**. Solari watches invite usage and attributes every join to the inviter.

## Settings

Dashboard → **Invite Tracking**: pick an optional log channel — every join posts there with the resolved inviter and their running invite count.

## Commands

| Command | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `/invites [user]` | How many members someone has invited |

Invite counts also power the **Invite members** achievement type, and rejoin loops don't inflate them — each member counts once per inviter.

> Solari needs the **Manage Server** permission to read the server's invites.
