# Tickets

Private support channels with transcripts and inactivity auto-close.

## How it works

1. Configure where tickets live and who handles them.
2. Deploy the **panel** — an embed with an "Open a ticket" button.
3. A member clicks it and gets a private channel visible to them and your support roles.
4. On close, a transcript can be posted to your transcript channel.

## Settings

Dashboard → **Tickets**:

| Section | Settings |
| --- | --- |
| Ticket Setup | Category for ticket channels, support roles, transcript channel |
| Ticket Behavior | Opening message, max open tickets per member, auto-close after N hours of inactivity |
| Ticket Panel | Title, description, button label, panel channel + deploy |

## Commands

| Command | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `/ticket` | Ticket controls (close, etc.) inside a ticket channel |
