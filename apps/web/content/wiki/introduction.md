# Introduction

**Solari** is an all-in-one Discord bot: moderation, auto-moderation, verification, leveling, achievements, tickets, giveaways, economy with a full casino, music, social alerts, and more — all managed from a fast web dashboard at [solari.gg](/).

## Why Solari?

- **One bot instead of ten.** Every module can be switched on or off per server, so you only run what you need.
- **Configure everything from the dashboard.** No memorizing setup commands — every option lives in the web panel, with role and channel pickers synced from your server.
- **Changes go live in about a second.** Saving a setting on the dashboard updates the live bot immediately, no restarts.
- **Per-command control.** Don't want `/rob` on your server? Turn off any single command from the dashboard.

## How this wiki is organized

- **Getting Started** — inviting the bot and finding your way around.
- **Module guides** — one page per module, matching the dashboard sidebar: what it does, its settings, and its commands.
- **Premium** — what the paid tier unlocks and how billing works.
