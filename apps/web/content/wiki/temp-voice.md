# Temp Voice

Join-to-create voice channels: members join a hub channel and get their own temporary room, cleaned up automatically when it empties. A Premium module.

## Setup

Dashboard → **Temp Voice**: pick the **hub** (join-to-create) channel and the category temporary rooms are created under.

## Commands

| Command | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `/voice` | Owner controls for your temporary room (lock, limit, rename, …) |
