# Suggestions

Collect ideas from members and triage them publicly.

## How it works

Members run `/suggest` — the suggestion posts to your configured channel with 👍/👎 voting. Staff mark suggestions as approved, considered, or denied, updating the embed in place.

## Commands

| Command | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `/suggest text` | Submit a suggestion |
| `/suggestion` | Staff triage (approve / consider / deny) |

## Settings

Dashboard → **Suggestions**: the suggestions channel and behavior options.
